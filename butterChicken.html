Ultimate Guide to Making Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is one of the most popular and beloved Indian dishes worldwide. Originating in Delhi, this rich, creamy, and mildly spiced curry pairs perfectly with naan, roti, or rice. In this detailed guide, we will cover the ingredients, preparation steps, cooking techniques, and essential tips to make the perfect butter chicken at home.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g (1 lb) boneless chicken thighs or breasts (thighs preferred for juiciness)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

For the Butter Chicken Gravy:

  • 3 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi, crushed)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (for deeper color and flavor)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional, for balancing flavors)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

  • Marinate the Chicken:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine all marinade ingredients.
    2. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well.
    3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  • Cook the Chicken:
    1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Cook the marinated chicken for 4-5 minutes per side.
    3. Remove from heat and set aside.
  • Prepare the Butter Chicken Sauce:
    1. Heat butter and oil over medium heat.
    2. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
    4. Pour in the tomato puree and cook for 10-12 minutes.
    5. Add spices and mix well.
    6. Add tomato paste, sugar, and water.
  • Blend the Sauce:
    1. Let the sauce cool slightly.
    2. Blend until smooth.
    3. Strain for a silky texture (optional).
  • Combine Chicken with the Sauce:
    1. Return the sauce to the pan.
    2. Add chicken and coat well.
    3. Stir in heavy cream and fenugreek leaves.
    4. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Final Touch and Serving:
    1. Adjust salt and spices.
    2. Garnish with coriander leaves.
    3. Serve with naan, roti, or rice.

Butter Chicken is an indulgent, creamy, and flavorful dish that’s easy to make at home with the right ingredients and techniques. Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve restaurant-quality results every time!
